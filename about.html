About Wingman Bahrain

Wingman Bahrain is a fast‑casual restaurant brand dedicated to delivering bold, flavour‑packed chicken wings made with high‑quality ingredients.

Our journey started with a passion for great food and a vision to create a brand that combines quality, consistency, and convenience.

Every wing is cooked to order using carefully selected ingredients and precise preparation techniques.

Serving wing lovers across Bahrain every week.
